Eau Claire Buddhist Sangha

Welcome

You are welcome here, whether you are new to Buddhism, new to meditation,
returning after time away, or simply curious.

All are welcome.

You do not need to identify as Buddhist, have prior experience, or wear special clothing. Chairs, cushions, and blankets are available.

What is a sangha?

A sangha is a community of practice. We come together to learn, sit, listen, ask questions, and support one another on the path.

  3. 7:30 PMDharma talk, Buddhist teaching, or discussion
  4. 8:00 PMQuestions, reflections, and community conversation

Arriving & Getting Settled

If you arrive after 7:00, please enter quietly and take any open seat. Chairs are available. Large cushions are under the table inside the hall; small cushions and blankets are in the closet.

Dana

We operate on dana, the Buddhist practice of generosity. There is no set fee for attendance.
