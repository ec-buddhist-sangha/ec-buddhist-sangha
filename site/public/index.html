Events • Jan 28, 2025

Weekly Tuesday Sit & Dharma Talk

Our core weekly gathering. We begin with 30 minutes of silent meditation, followed by a talk on “The Second Noble Truth: The Cause of Suffering”.

Schedule

  • 6:45 PM - Optional meditation instruction for beginners
  • 7:00 PM - Silent sitting meditation (30 minutes)
  • 7:30 PM - Dharma talk or discussion
  • 8:00 PM - Tea and open discussion

All levels of experience are welcome. Cushions and chairs are provided. No special clothing needed.

We operate on Dana (generosity). There is no set fee for attendance.

Announcements • Jan 19, 2025

New Library Books Available

We have added five new titles to our community library, including Pema Chödrön’s latest work on living with compassion and uncertainty.

New titles include:

  • Living Beautifully with Uncertainty and Change by Pema Chödrön
  • The Art of Gathering by Priya Krishna
  • Stillness Speaks by Eckhart Tolle

Feel free to browse and borrow after our Tuesday meetings. Our library is a free resource for all sangha members.
