{{< sangha-box title="Dana: The Practice of Generosity" >}}
Dana is the Pali word for generosity — one of the foundational practices in Buddhism. Our sangha is sustained entirely through dana.

There is no fee to attend the weekly gatherings. All are invited to contribute what they can — whether through time, talent, or financial support — toward the flourishing of this community.
{{< /sangha-box >}}

## What Your Donations Support

- Room rental at Unity of Eau Claire
- Meditation cushions, chairs, and blankets
- Library materials and dharma resources
- Dharma talk recordings and media
