I'd like to propose we read **"The Heart of the Buddha's Teaching"** by Thich Nhat Hanh for our January/February book club cycle.

## Why this book?

- It's accessible to newcomers but has depth for experienced practitioners
- Thich Nhat Hanh's writing style is clear and practical
- It covers the core teachings in a way that connects to everyday life

## Suggested schedule

- **Week 1-2:** Part One — The Four Noble Truths (chapters 1-7)
- **Week 3-4:** Part Two — The Noble Eightfold Path (chapters 8-16)
- **Week 5-6:** Part Three — Other Buddhist Teachings (chapters 17-28)

We could discuss each section during our Wednesday evening sits. What does everyone think of this timeline?

If you've read this book before, please share what stood out to you. If you're new to Thich Nhat Hanh, you're in for a treat!
